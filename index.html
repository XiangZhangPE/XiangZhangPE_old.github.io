Introduction

I'm a Power Electronics M.A.Sc. candidate at Queen's University's Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, working under the mentorship of Dr. Praveen K. Jain and Dr. Shangzhi Pan.

My research involves coupled magnetics application in resonant converters, current sharing techniques of multiphase /multimodule converter, and high precise digital control.

Prior to joining the M.A.Sc. program, I completed my bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Wuhan University advised by Dr. Meng Huang.

Outside of academia, I like photographing, aerial filming, and soul-like games.
